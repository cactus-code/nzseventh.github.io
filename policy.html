Our Policy

Immigration

We love immigrants! That's why, if elected, we will make sure we get the best ones. Far form being homophobic, we support a homogenous society.

Immigrants will be selected based on:

  • Bungee-jumping ability
  • Ability to articulate why Western democracy is the best societal model
  • Religious belief, or, of course, secular belief in religous belief
  • Proficiency using Edmonds recipes
  • Enjoyment of Pavlova, and belief in its origin in New Zealand

These criteria will be tested using an NCEA-derived exam, and failure to meet them will result in immediate deportation.

New immigrants will have to undergo a short (2 years) run-up period before offical residency. During this time they may be hunted as game.

We will also provide a second tier of citizenship for new citizens, specially tailored towards their needs. Notable differences will include:

  • A customised passport with visas for only the essentail foreign states
  • A special "Good foreigner" badge
  • No cumbersome welfare - they made it here, didn't they?

The Environment

NZ Seventh believes in the environment. It most likely exists! If we get into government, we will form a special committee to verify the environment’s existence. We will also investigate options for implementing the words “environment”, “renewables”, and “climate change” in speeches. We are committed to appearing to care about environmental issues, and unlike the major national parties, will not clutter our beliefs with cumbersome policy.
